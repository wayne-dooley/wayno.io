 My Favorites
 
The Dilbert Zone. This guy must have a hidden camera in my office building.

WebMonkey. An exceptional online magazine with hot tips and the latest information on the web and HTML programming techniques. In other words, this is where I steal a lot of ideas from. A feature of magazine.
 
 
The Tombstone Tourist. Did your favorite movie star die? Not sure? This is the place! Lists a celebrity tombstone of the month, with a short summary of their often too short life. This site was slammed by Kathy Lee, which makes it that much more appealing.
